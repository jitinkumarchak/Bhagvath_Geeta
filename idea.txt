# Core Product Idea

**An AI-powered Bhagavad Gita platform** that allows users to:

1. **Read the Bhagavad Gita in a beautiful book-like experience**
2. **Understand every verse using AI explanations**
3. **Have conversations with an AI Gita teacher**
4. **Get life guidance based on Gita wisdom**

The platform combines **scripture + AI + modern UX**.

---

# Core Content Structure

The Bhagavad Gita contains:

* **18 Chapters**
* **700 Verses**

Each verse page will include:

1. Sanskrit verse
2. Transliteration
3. English translation
4. Optional commentary
5. AI explanation/chat option

Example structure:

```
Chapter 2 – Sankhya Yoga

Verse 47

Sanskrit
कर्मण्येवाधिकारस्ते मा फलेषु कदाचन

Transliteration
Karmanye vadhikaraste ma phaleshu kadachana

Translation
You have the right to perform your duties, but not to the fruits of your actions.
```

# Main Platform Features

## 1. Gita Reader

Users can read the entire Bhagavad Gita.

Features:

* Chapter navigation
* Verse navigation
* Clean typography
* Sanskrit + translation
* Next/previous verse

Future upgrade:

* 3D page-flipping book experience.

---

## 2. AI Verse Explanation

Every verse will have an **“Ask AI” option**.

Users can ask questions like:

* Explain this verse in simple terms
* What does this mean in modern life?
* Give an example
* What philosophy does this represent?

AI responds with contextual explanations.

---

## 3. Conversational Gita AI

Users can chat with the AI like a **personal spiritual teacher**.

Example conversations:

User:
“I am stressed about my career.”

AI:
Responds using relevant Gita teachings and verses.

Supported conversation types:

* Verse discussion
* Life advice using Gita philosophy
* Philosophy questions

Examples:

* What is karma?
* What is dharma?
* What does the Gita say about death?

---

## 4. Life Situation → Verse Engine

Users select their current struggle.

Example options:

* Anxiety
* Fear of failure
* Career confusion
* Anger
* Overthinking
* Loss

The system returns a relevant verse and explanation.

Example:

Fear of failure → Chapter 2 Verse 47.

---

## 5. Daily Wisdom

Users receive a **daily verse**.

Shows:

* reflection.

This can later support **daily notifications**.

# AI System Features

## 1. Verse-Aware AI

AI answers based on specific verses.

The verse context is passed to the model so answers remain relevant.

---

## 2. Retrieval System (RAG)

AI retrieves relevant verses before answering.

Example flow:

User question:

“How do I deal with failure?”

System finds verses such as:

* Chapter 2 Verse 47
* Chapter 2 Verse 48

Then generates the answer.

---

## 3. Multi-Turn Conversations

Users can continue chatting with the AI.

Conversation history is stored.

Example:

User: Explain this verse
User: Give an example
User: How does this apply to work?
